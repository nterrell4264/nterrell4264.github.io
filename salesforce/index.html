About Me

My name is Nathan Terrell. I'm a Salesforce Certified Platform Developer 1 and Administrator with a university degree in Game Design and Development from the Rochester Institute of Technology.

The amount of overlap between these two areas will surprise you: Video game levels are built with Objects, these objects react to the player character using automated logic like Flows, these reactions create a user experience for the player, and these experiences are tracked as stats and iterated upon like an Opportunity worked with a Dashboard.

My passion is in analyzing distributions and combinations, and Salesforce is a great tool for doing so, whether within the realm of video games or not. That's why I've pushed and gotten two certifications within just six months of learning the platform!
