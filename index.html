About Me

Programming. Design. Writing. Music. Art. These are the industrial pillars that video games are built on, and one day, I plan to master them all.

My name is Nathan Terrell. I'm a proud Coloradoan living in Austin, TX, with a Bachelor's of Arts and Science in Game Creation and Application of Irish Storytelling from the Rochester Institute of Technology. This degree is personalized through RIT's School of Individualized Study, reflecting my unique studies in game design, game development, and a study abroad in Cork, Ireland.

The crafted projects you'll find below have evolved from the spreadsheets, stories, and digital art of my adolescence, which themselves spring from a childhood of simple lists and drawings on a clipboard. The building blocks of video games have always been as much of a physical and intellectual engagement to me as a car is to a mechanic or a bird is to an ornithologist. On the occasions where I'm not making, playing, or thinking about video games, I enjoy philosophy and creative writing.
